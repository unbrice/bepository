- AI tools are welcome as an aid.
- A human must vouch for every change merged under their name: they have read
  the full diff and take responsibility for it. CI passing is necessary but
  not sufficient.
- The submitter must understand and be able to explain every change they
  submit, in their own words.
- Substantially AI-generated changes (as opposed to AI-assisted ones) must be
  flagged in the PR description so reviewers can calibrate their scrutiny.
- Autonomous agents may prepare and ship changes (e.g. via `just ship`), but
  the human who triggers the ship carries the same obligations as above.
- Attributing specific models in the commit message is fine but not required.
